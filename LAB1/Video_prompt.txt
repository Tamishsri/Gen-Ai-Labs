Act as a professional video content designer creating promotional videos
for university-level entrepreneurship and innovation events.

Generate a short promotional video composed of 10 continuous scenes for a
Two-Day National-Level Pitch Deck & Startup Idea Competition for Young Entrepreneurs
hosted by RV University.

The video should follow a logical narrative flow, starting with the challenges
faced by aspiring entrepreneurs and idea-stage founders, gradually transitioning
to innovation, startup thinking, and pitching solutions, and finally inviting
students to participate in the competition.

Scene Progression and Content:

1. Introduce the problem of young innovators having ideas but lacking a platform
   to showcase them.
2. Highlight common challenges such as unclear business models, fear of failure,
   and lack of mentorship or exposure.
3. Emphasize that many students do not know how to convert ideas into viable startups.
4. Introduce entrepreneurship, innovation, and pitch decks as powerful tools
   to transform ideas into real-world solutions.
5. Announce that RV University is organizing a National-Level Pitch Deck &
   Startup Idea Competition for young entrepreneurs.
6. Highlight that the event is a two-day, hands-on, on-campus competition scheduled
   for 20–21 February 2025.
7. State eligibility by inviting undergraduate and postgraduate students from
   all departments to participate individually or in teams of 1–4 members.
8. Emphasize the availability of attractive cash prizes (₹30,000, ₹20,000, ₹15,000),
   along with merit awards and participation certificates.
9. Inform viewers that registration is completely free and closes on
   15 February 2025.
10. Conclude with a strong call-to-action encouraging students to scan the QR code
    and register, inviting them to pitch their ideas at RV University,
    Mysore Road Campus, Bengaluru.

Visual Style:
• Modern, startup-focused visuals
• Elements such as pitch decks, charts, graphs, lightbulbs, growth arrows,
  and innovation icons
• Clean, minimal backgrounds with subtle futuristic UI overlays
• Smooth cinematic transitions between scenes
• Clear and readable text overlays supporting the narration

Tone and Vibe:
Inspirational, motivating, student-centric, professional, and future-oriented

Video Length:
Short promotional video (30–45 seconds)

Output Format:
Vertical or landscape video suitable for social media, email campaigns,
and university promotions
